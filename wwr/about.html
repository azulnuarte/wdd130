Rafting Mendoza

We want to provide thrilling rafting experiences in the beautiful landscapes of Mendoza, fostering a love for adventure and nature.

History

The Rise of "Mendoza Rapids Adventures" Diego Martinez, a Mendoza native and passionate river guide, envisioned sharing the thrill of rafting with others. Teaming up with childhood friend Sofia Ramirez, they founded "Mendoza Rapids Adventures" in 2010. Their commitment to safety, sustainability, and service quickly made them the go-to rafting outfitter in South America. Legacy and Vision Diego and Sofia's company expanded to offer various outdoor activities while championing environmental conservation. Today, "Mendoza Rapids Adventures" stands as a testament to their dream, inspiring adventurers worldwide while protecting Mendoza's natural heritage.
