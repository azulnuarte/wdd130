Mendoza

Mendoza is a city in Argentina, characterized by its beautiful mountains and varied climate. It is a very famous tourist spot in Argentina, the Andes divide this beautiful city from the country of Chile. It is an arid area and is also characterized by the zonda wind. It is also a city famous for its vineyards and the wine that is exported..
